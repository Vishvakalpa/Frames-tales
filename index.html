Submission Requirements

  • Send us your best-loved click.
  • Include a short story (1–3 sentences) explaining why it's special to you
  • Minimum resolution: 1200px on the longer side
  • Accepted formats: JPEG or PNG
  • Submit Good quality picture clicked by you only.

Restrictions

  • No identifiable human portraits
  • No NSFW or explicit content
  • No hate speech or abusive material
  • Don't use someone else's click
  • Using of photos clicked using snapchats are not allowed
  • Use of excessive filters are not allowed
